1. The CSS means cascading style sheet. it is used for styling the webpage with color ,animation and custom layout.
CSS Selectors are type, id, class, group and universal.
2.The CSS box model is used for the layout. it consist of the margin, border, padding and content.
-content :- the content is where the text ,image and other content related things will be placed in it. it is also in the center of the box model.
-padding :- the padding is the transparent space between the border and the content.
-border :- the border is a line at the edge of the padding.
-margin :- the margin is the transparent space at the outside layer of the box model.
